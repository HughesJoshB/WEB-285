Benefits of joining the GFAA

The GFAA exists to promote the professionalism, education, career development and pro-active legislative efforts to benefit owners, investors, residents and association members in our territories. Your membership with the GFAA is a solid investment for your profession, your company, and the apartment industry. We are dedicated to promoting and advancing the needs of the apartment industry in this market. Our members include apartment communities, apartment owners/management companies, suppliers and vendors, and professionals right here in the Greater Fayetteville area!

Here are just a few of the benefits you receive as an GFAA member:
  • Networking, making contacts, member roster, get referrals from our website
  • Exposure by a listing in our directory online-- you can add links to your website, all social media devices, coupons and more!
  • Discounted pricing on events and classes, and some events we hold are Members Only!!
  • Access to career resources including job postings
  • Educational opportunities, designation courses, maintenance certifications

Owner and Manager Benefits

The GFAA can help you develop your portfolio in so many ways! From networking, marketing opportunities, and educational classes for earning and maintaining designations for our industry, you will have many benefits by joining today.

Management Companies - NEW!! We now offer a membership to Management Companies. The annual fee is only $250.00. For this cost your company becomes a member of the Greater Fayetteville Apartment Association, and you and your staff can participate in our events and seminars, even your employees from other markets that fall under your company. The Management Company membership is a solution for Regionals and Executives to keep their expenses for their own education and networking separated from the budgets from those on-site. You may also post FREE employment ads through our website if you are need of new staffing at any level. This is also a great membership option for local companies that manage single family homes!

Apartment Communities There are many apartment communities in our market that are members of the Greater Fayetteville Apartment Association. Dues are charged annually in January, and based on tiers by number of units. In addition to the benefits of networking and educational opportunities, you also have access to the Blue Moon lease agreement, and you may choose to use either the AANC or the NAA version. You may also post FREE employment ads on our website for 30 days if you are need of on-site help!

Annual Fee Structure for Apartment Communities
  • 0-100 units $200.00 plus $1.60 per unit
  • 101-249 units $350.00 plus $1.60 per unit
  • 250 + units $450.00 plus $1.60 per unit

Supplier/Vendor Benefits

Need new ways to meet people to help grow your business? Joining the Greater Fayetteville Apartment Association is a great way to expand your contacts database and find new clientele! We offer many opportunities for you to get your name out there:

  • Attending monthly general membership breakfasts and luncheons
  • Having a booth at the annual Tradeshow
  • Joining a committee
  • Sponsoring a class/seminar
  • Sponsoring an event
  • Stepping up your membership to next level for more exposure
  • Spotlight speaker position
  • Networking and social events
  • Post FREE employment ads on our website for 30 days if you are need of staff
  • Online recognition in our database that all members can search for the services you provide
  • Advertising Opportunities on our website - Coming Soon!!

Breakfast/Lunches are FREE for one employee from your business at every general membership meeting, may choose ONE major event for a FREE table in addition to your Tradeshow Booth (Casino Night/Membership Drive, or Maintenance Mania, etc, excludes JAC Awards.) Also receive a FREE home page position on this website with a link to your website! Additional costs accrue seminars and sponsoring events and/or classes
  • Platinum Membership $2000.00 per year - (space is limited for this sponsorship level), access to website and member directory, listed in member directory, invited to all events. Breakfast/Lunches are FREE for one employee from your business at every general membership meeting, ALL major events guarantee a FREE table (only excludes JAC Awards). Also receive a FREE home page position on this website with a link to your business! Additional costs only accrue for attending and/or sponsoring seminars
